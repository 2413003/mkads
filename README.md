# mkads
ad platform better for consumers, advertisers, venues/hosts. (all key stakeholders)

consumers: less quantity, higher relevance, only actually truly helpful ads for things they actually truly want that solve actual real problems they have. Big highest priority/value problems. Not invasive/annoying. Actually helpful & useful and something people actually like, not dislike & put up with/try to skip. makes local events more sustainable & supported at no extra cost to consumers. lowering prices & supporting community venues & events alive at no extra cost.

for advertisers: higher ROI. supporting community. 

venues: monetise their property (ad rev shared), grow revenue from existing property at no extra cost
